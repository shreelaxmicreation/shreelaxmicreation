Leading Shirting Fabric Manufacturer Ahmedabad

Shree Laxmi Creation is a premier shirting fabric manufacturer Ahmedabad based, specializing in economical and mid-range shirting fabrics. With a rich heritage in textile manufacturing, we have established ourselves as a trusted bulk shirting fabric supplier in India, delivering unmatched quality and value to our clients.

Our extensive portfolio features over 50 fabric bases and 500+ design options. We meticulously craft a diverse range of fabric types, including premium cotton, poplin, oxford, dobby, herringbone, twill, birdseye, and versatile poly-cotton blends. Whether you are looking for classic weaves or contemporary textures, our collection caters to every stylistic requirement.

Quality begins at the source. Our dedicated greige production facility is located in Ichalkaranji—the renowned textile hub—ensuring stringent control over the foundational weave. The fabric then undergoes advanced processing in Ahmedabad and Mumbai, utilizing state-of-the-art technology to achieve impeccable finishes, vibrant colors, and superior hand-feel.

As a leading shirting fabric supplier India relies on, we proudly serve apparel brands, garment manufacturers, and wholesale buyers both domestically and internationally. Our commitment to timely delivery, consistent quality, and competitive pricing makes Shree Laxmi Creation the definitive sourcing partner for your bulk fabric needs.
